About Me


Iuro Nascimento is a Ph.D. student at Federal University of Minas Gerais and is part of MACRO research group. His research interest includes robotics, control systems, predictive controllers and artificial inteligence.




Interests

  • Nonlinear Model Predictive Control
  • Robotics
  • Nonlinear Optimization
  • Obstacle Avoidance
  • Artificial Inteligence

Education

  • Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering, 2020- in progress
  • M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering, 2019
  • B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering, 2016
